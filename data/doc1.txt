Title: Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR)
Section 2.1: All personal data must be processed lawfully, fairly, and in a transparent manner.
Section 2.5: Data controllers must implement adequate security measures to protect data against unauthorized access or disclosure.
Section 3.2: Individuals have the right to access and correct their personal data upon request.
Enforcement: The Nigeria Data Protection Bureau (NDPB) shall monitor compliance and apply sanctions for violations.
